Why interview.io?

Problem

  • The job interview process has become lengthy and complicated.
  • Job seekers typically apply for dozens of positions and wait for weeks or even months to hear back from the companies.
  • Job interview process management can be complicated when candidates receive multiple interview invitations.

Solution

How It Works

1Connect to your current email/calendar account
2Find and sync up ongoing job interviews
3Aggregate, predict, and display essential information

Features

All your essential interview information at a glance

After syncing up with your email/calendar, interview.io displays all you need to know about your upcoming interviews in one place.

Go above and beyond just time and location

Using ML and third-party database (e.g. Glassdoor, Quora, etc.), interview.io predicts the interview process timeline and incorporates tips to prepare for a successful interview.
